How They Show Love

Physical, intense, and impossible to ignore. An Aries pet loves you like a freight train — full speed, no brakes. They'll body-slam you with affection, follow you everywhere, and physically place themselves between you and anything they consider a threat. Their love language is presence. Aggressive, enthusiastic presence.

Aries pets wilt without stimulation. They need new routes, new challenges, new things to conquer. A bored Aries is a destructive Aries. Give them a mission and they'll give you their whole heart.
