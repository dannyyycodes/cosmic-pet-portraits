A Leo Dog — a dog born between Jul 23 – Aug 22 — is dramatic, generous, and fully aware of how good they look. A Leo pet doesn't want your attention — they require it. The Leo Dog soul, in a nutshell

Dramatic, generous, and fully aware of how good they look. A Leo pet doesn't want your attention — they require it. Fire-sign dogs run hot: they're the ones turning a quiet walk into a full sprint and greeting you like you've returned from war.

Core traits:

  • Dramatic
  • Warm-hearted
  • Proud
  • Generous
  • Attention-loving

You know your dog is a Leo when…

  • Has an actual signature pose they strike when they know you're watching
  • Will sulk — visibly, dramatically — if they feel ignored
  • Positions themselves in the exact centre of any room, group, or photo
  • Greets guests like a celebrity greeting fans
  • Has never once been subtle about anything in their entire life

How They Show Love

Grand, warm, and impossible to miss. A Leo pet loves you like you're their favourite person in the world (because you are, and they need you to know it). Their affection is big, warm, and public. They'll show off for you, protect you, and make you feel like the most important human alive.

  • Performs for you — tricks, rolls, zoomies — and looks for applause
  • Puts their paw on you possessively when others are around
  • Literally beams when you praise them

The One Thing They Need From You: Recognition

Leo pets need to feel special. Tell them they're beautiful. Praise them when they do literally anything. A Leo who feels seen is the most loyal, generous, joyful companion alive. A Leo who feels ignored will make sure you regret it.
