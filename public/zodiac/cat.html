Cat zodiac — common questions

How do I find out my cat's zodiac sign?

Use your cat's date of birth (or adoption date if unknown) and match it to the sign date ranges. A cat born March 21–April 19 is an Aries, April 20–May 20 a Taurus, and so on through the twelve signs.

Do cats really have zodiac signs?

Every cat is born under the same sky as any of us, so the same tropical zodiac applies. Their sun sign captures their core temperament; a full birth chart adds their moon (emotional nature) and rising sign (how they meet the world).

What if I don't know my cat's birthday?

Rescue and shelter cats often have an estimated or adoption date — that 'gotcha day' works as a soul birthday and still produces a meaningful chart. Little Souls readings handle unknown birth times gracefully.
