Before You Order

Is this actually personal to my pet, or is it generic?
Every reading is generated from your pet's exact birth date, name, and photo. The astrology uses a professional-grade ephemeris to calculate their precise planetary positions — so two pets born on the same day get completely different readings. Real data, real personality, not a template.
How long does it take to receive?
Most readings land in your inbox within 5–10 minutes. Occasionally up to 20 minutes during busy periods. You'll get a confirmation email first, then a second email with your full reading as a downloadable PDF.
What if I don't know my pet's exact birth date?
An approximate date works fine — even just the month and year from your adoption records or vet notes. Rescue pets get beautiful readings too. Just use your best estimate.
What animals can I get a reading for?
Dogs, cats, rabbits, horses, birds, reptiles, fish — any animal you love. We also do memorial readings for pets who have crossed the rainbow bridge, as a way to honour their memory.
What if I'm not happy with it?
We have a 100% money-back guarantee. If your reading doesn't move you — if it doesn't feel true to your pet — email us and we'll refund you in full, no questions asked. We stand behind every reading completely.
Can I order one as a gift?
Absolutely — it's one of the most personal gifts you can give a pet owner. Just enter their pet's details and put the recipient's email address. They'll receive the full reading directly. People screenshot and share these constantly.
